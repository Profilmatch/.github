## Who are we?
**Profil Match** is a Danish staffing agency with over 20 years of experience (est. 2002). We are a dedicated team that believes recruitment is about more than just paperwork—it’s about people, trust, and finding the perfect professional fit.

## What do we do?
We bridge the gap between talented workers and growing businesses. Simply put:
* **For Job Seekers:** We offer flexible jobs in sectors like logistics, construction, and service, while taking full care of your contracts and payroll.
* **For Companies:** We provide reliable temporary staff on short notice to help you handle busy periods (like Black Friday or Christmas) without the stress.

In short: **We create the right match so both people and businesses can grow.**
